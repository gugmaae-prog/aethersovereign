Founder & Chair — Aether Sovereign

A Spanish professional with a distinguished career spanning Europe, the GCC, the US, and the UK, I merge longevity, regenerative medicine, technology, decarbonized structures, and strategic economic execution to drive systemic impact. My work focuses on urban development, national infrastructure, and cultural and health systems innovation — successfully bridging private breakthroughs with public governance.

I architect holistic solutions across smart mobility, green energy, smart hospitals, proptech, maritime logistics, digital infrastructure, and cultural ecosystems to elevate both communities and nations. Our strategy emphasizes long-term vision, actionable insights, and high-level collaboration with sovereign entities, private investors, and global innovators to foster sustainable growth and regional leadership.

Westfield, Phase 2

Seventy new retail units including a large John Lewis anchor store. A complex steel frame using 15,000 tonnes of steel, spanning the Central line infrastructure with heavy plate girders, trusses, and hanging structures.

02 · Yas Island, Abu Dhabi
Ferrari World Abu Dhabi

Masterplanning, urban design, landscape, and architecture. 176,000m² with Aldar Properties. A cultural landmark — the first Ferrari Theme Park, requiring bold creative vision while honouring the iconic brand.

03 · Segrate, Milan
Westfield Milan

The largest new shopping centre in Europe on a 60-hectare site. Approximately 380 stores, a luxury village, leisure, entertainment, dining, and 10,000 parking spaces.

04 · Knightsbridge, London
The Mandarin Oriental

Construction management through Arc Atelier Group delivering this 200-bedroom landmark luxury hotel — a prestigious beacon redefining the hospitality landscape in London's most iconic address.

05 · Palm Jumeirah, Dubai
St Regis The Palm

The hotel occupies the first 18 floors of Palm Tower at the heart of Palm Jumeirah. Adjacent to the Palm Monorail, minutes from Aquaventure and The Pointe's waterfront dining.

06 · Mushaireb, Doha
Banyan Tree Doha

Five-star luxury property designed by Jacques Garcia, opened May 2021. The first property under the Banyan Tree–Accor strategic alliance, anchoring the cultural heart of Doha's historic Mushaireb district.

07 · Downtown Dubai
St Regis Financial Road

A 60-story tower with views of Burj Khalifa and the Dubai Canal. 232 units, 1–5 bedrooms. Inspired by areesh houses, ghaf trees, falaj systems, pearl diving, and Arabian coffee — rooted in UAE heritage.

08 · Deira, Dubai
Jewel of the Creek

One million sqm mixed-use development at the north shore of Dubai Creek. Transforming Port Saeed into a vibrant urban centre incorporating residential, commercial, hospitality, and recreational uses.
